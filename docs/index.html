what is welt?

In a universe where anything is possible, it's your job to explore farther than the reaches of what you ever thought was possible.

From the beginning you'll be surrounded with any resource needed for you to move up in the tiers of science or alchemy. But remember, once you make your choice, you can never go back.

From exploring the local system to discovering what lies beyond your galaxy, you'll find systems flourishing with life, both friendly and hostile. Whether you befriend or betray the civilizations you encounter, your choices will determine the fate of reality itself.
